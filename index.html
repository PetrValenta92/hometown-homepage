Visit Prague

Enjoy history, culture and entertainment in the city which is not too small to be boring and not too large to be annoying.

Top three activities to do at Prague

Expand your knowledge

Prague is not only "castle and bridge". It is one of the oldest cities in Eastern Europe with thousand years long history.

Enrich your soul

Theaters, concerts, museums, galleries, expositions, sport events and many many more are happening everyday, everynight.

Satisfy you tummy

In Prague you can find dishes just like our grandmothers did, as well as international cuisine. And beer of course!
